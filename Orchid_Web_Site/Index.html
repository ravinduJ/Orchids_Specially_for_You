The orchid is Mother Nature's masterpiece.

Robyn

This orchid project was a brain child of the CEO. He is on retirement after a long spell of holding many Private Sector & Public Sector appointments. He started this project at the end of 2018. He selected the ideal climatic condition for this project being his home base. Its 149 km(04 hrs) from Colombo.Altitude:1998.03ft above sea level and mostly dry climate in the intermediate zone. Water is available in abundance. Ideally conditions for growing Orchids, Organic Vegetable & Foliage. The land use for the project is in the large garden of the CEO's Ancestral home which is about 200 years old. Where Adigars & Chieftan live in the past and govern this part of the country in the olden days. At the moment the Orchids are grown in the most orderly built net covered enclosure (150ft x 45ft) it hoses approximately 13,200 Potted orchids of Sonia Red and Sonia White from the Dendrobium family.In the very near future the 2nd net house will be built of the same size as 1st net house and More Dendrobiums,Oncidiums, Vandas,....................... will be grown fore Orchid lovers to buy cut flowers on orders. We have a good Island wide courier service for cut flower delivery. Minimum orders excepted for deliver will be 100 flowers(approx.20 sprigs) packed in corrugated cartons. Our service will be "Orchids Specially for you".
